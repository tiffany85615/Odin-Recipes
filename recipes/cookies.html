Chocolate Chip Cookies Recipe

This is a recipe for amazing chocolate chip cookies.

They are easy to make and are soft and rich. They are a perfect desert for any occasion.

Ingredients:

  • butter
  • white sugar
  • brown sugar
  • eggs
  • vanilla
  • baking soda
  • hot water
  • salt
  • flour
  • chocolate chips
  • walnuts (optional)

Steps:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350Â°F
  2. Beat the butter and sugars together until smooth, then beat in the eggs one at a time, and stir in the vanilla
  3. dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add it along with the salt to the batter
  4. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts
  5. drop spoonfuls of the dough 2 inches apart onto baking sheets
  6. bake for about 10 minutes or until edges are golden brown
  7. let cool completely
