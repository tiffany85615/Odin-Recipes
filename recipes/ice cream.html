Vanilla Ice Cream Recipe

This is a recipe for delicious homemade vanilla ice cream.

It is very sweet and creamy, perfect for special occasions.

Ingredients:

  • heavy whipping cream
  • half-and-half cream
  • sugar
  • vanilla

Steps:

  1. Combine all ingrediants until the sugar is completely dissolved
  2. Fill an ice cream maker two-thirds full and freeze according to the manufacturer's instructions
  3. serve immediately or store in freezer
